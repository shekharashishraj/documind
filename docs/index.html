Overview

Why MalDoc?

MalDoc targets a practical blind spot: PDF parsers and agentic systems often see different content than a human reader. MalDoc orchestrates structured extraction, vulnerability-focused analysis, attack planning, and controlled injection to study how downstream systems can be influenced by adversarial documents.

Pipeline

Multi-Stage Adversarial Pipeline

The pipeline runs five stages: extraction, GPT-based analysis, manipulation planning, PDF injection/overlay, and agentic vulnerability evaluation. Each stage writes structured artifacts for reproducibility and ablation studies.

Dataset

Documents & Attack Scenarios

MalDoc supports structured PDF inputs spanning reports, policies, and transactional forms. Each document is paired with a scenario-driven evaluation prompt and threat model.

  • Multi-format parsing: byte extraction, OCR, and VLM outputs.
  • Attack planning with explicit scopes, payloads, and consistency notes.
  • Agentic evaluation with multi-trial scoring and majority vote.
