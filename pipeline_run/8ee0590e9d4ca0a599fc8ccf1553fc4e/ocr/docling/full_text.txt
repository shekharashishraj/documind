## Dear participants of the Wikimedia Conference 2018,

Please take a few minutes to give us your valuable feedback on the conference! By filling in this feedback form, you will help to improve the quality and impact of the Wikimedia Conference in the future. We explicitly encourage you to express also critical feedback - if applicable - to gain a realistic picture of the strengths or weaknesses of the conference. The data from this survey will be processed statistically and the results will be published only in an aggregated form, which means that there will be no possibility to identify you or your individual answers. All responses are optional.
